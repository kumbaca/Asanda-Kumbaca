I’m a multi-skilled Graphic Designer Specialist with 11+ years of experience across retail, publishing, and sports betting. My journey has been more than design — it’s been about finding creative ways to push brands forward.

At SunBet, I pioneered AI-driven motion graphics, transforming static “Big Win” posts into dynamic animated campaigns that boosted engagement.

I became a standard part of the brand’s content strategy. At Flash, I introduced 3D parallax animation to create motion without video budgets, showing how innovation can stretch possibilities.

At Conde Nast, I delivered an animated magazine cover by creatively combining outdated CS5 software with my personal devices — proving adaptability and resilience under pressure.

I thrive where creativity meets technology and strategy — blending design, animation, and marketing to deliver results that last.

My goal is to keep evolving into a hybrid creative professional who can shape campaigns, lead innovation, and make ideas come alive across all platforms.
